Frequently Asked Questions

What is a night at Flipside like?

A night at Flipside is an opportunity for you to unleash your inner creative. Choose what speaks to you: drawing live models? Creating pottery? Wielding a paintbrush? And, of course, we know a perfect night isn’t complete without your favourite bottle of wine — enjoy a glass (or two) while one of our artists takes you step-by-step through the process. Whether you’re a closet Monet or a daytime doodler, we provide everything you need for a relaxing, feel-good evening of bringing your imagination to life.

What types of activities can I find at a Flipside event?

No matter your craft of choice, we’ve got you covered. More traditional art classes will feature canvas paintings with mediums like acrylics and oils, watercolours on paper, charcoal drawings, and life and still sketches. Floral fanatics can get their fill with our flower arrangement classes, while you can also flex your green thumb at our terrarium making events. Check out our Events page to broaden your creative horizons.

What is included in the cost of the session?

Everything you need to channel your inner Picasso. Sips and snacks can be purchased from our lovely venue partners.

Should I make a booking in advance?

Yes! We only have limited seats for each session, so be sure to make a booking in advance. Reserve your spot here.

What time should I arrive and how long are the sessions?

To have time to settle in and choose a bottle of wine, we recommend getting there five to 10 minutes before it starts. Each session will last two hours.

If I come with a group or a friend, can we sit together?

Of course! Once you make your online reservation, please send us an email with your booking number and your requested seating arrangement.

What do I wear to a night at Flipside?

Whatever you want! We supply aprons, although to avoid any laundry dramas, we recommend packing a spare shirt or outfit that you don’t mind getting paint on.

Do I have to create the same thing as the artist that evening?

Definitely not! Flipside events are all about letting your creative genius run free. While we give you the tools and the guidance of a talented instructor, what you do with your masterpiece is completely up to you.

What if I need to cancel my booking?

Please make changes or cancellations within the 48-hour window. After that, you will incur a $30 non-refundable cancellation fee.

Can I purchase wine or beer at Flipside?

Our venue partners have a delightful selection of wine, cocktails, and food available for purchase.

Can I host a private party or function at Flipside?

Yes — we love a good party! We can accommodate whether you’re celebrating a corporate event, a birthday, end-of-year festivities, a hens party, or just looking for an unforgettable night. Just shoot us an email with the group details and desired session, and we’ll start putting together your Flispide event.

Can I purchase a gift voucher?

How thoughtful of you! We’re more than happy to help. Head over to our Gift Cards page to design and send an e-gift card to the lucky person of your choice. You will receive a confirmation email with the details of your purchase.

Are children allowed at the events?

All Flipside attendees must be at least 18 years of age.

Can we BYO our own bites and nibbles?

As our venue partners offer such a delicious selection, you are unable to bring your own.
